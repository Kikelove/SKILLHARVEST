Our Objective

Comprehensive strategies that drive enteprise success.

  • Educating Entreprenuers

    This education typically covers a wide range of topics related to starting, managing, and growing businesses, including governance, planning, marketing, finance, leadership, and problem-solving.

  • Engaging Entrepreneurs

    Actively engaging and dialoging with individuals, Nano, Micro, Small, Medium and Corporate Enterprises.

  • Creating socioeconomic value

    It involves initiatives, activities and strategies undertaken by individuals and organisations with the aim of improving the wellbeing, prosperity and success of enterprises

  • Collaborating with organisations

    working together towards common goals, shared resources, and combine expertise and efforts to achieve mutually beneficial outcomes.

  • Promoting Good Governance

    Partnering with institutions in the public, private and Non-profit Organisations for establishing and practising best governance practices principles for entrepreneurial success

  • Capacity Development

    Improving capacity for Entrepreneurs through research and development initiatives of the Centre.

  • Youth Empowerment Programme

    Promoting sustainable youth empowerment programme in Africa.

  • Diversity and Inclusion

    Driving equal opportunities to create value for all and sundry.
